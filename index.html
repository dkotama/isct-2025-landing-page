The International Symposium on Consumer Technologies (ISCT) is the premier global event organized by the IEEE Consumer Technology Society (CTSoc). Since its establishment, ISCT has brought together a diverse international audience, including researchers, industry leaders, academics, and students, to explore groundbreaking advances in consumer technology. This symposium serves as a vital forum for presenting innovative concepts, learning about emerging trends, and exchanging ideas on consumer-focused technology with an emphasis on sustainability and environmental impact.

The 2025 edition of ISCT will again take place in Bali, Indonesia, continuing the legacy established in its inaugural conference in 2024. Bali, known for its rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty, offers a unique backdrop for professionals to connect and collaborate on transformative consumer technologies in a sustainable environment.

Accepted papers will be considered for inclusion in IEEE Xplore, following IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality standards. Top papers will receive invitations to submit to special editions of IEEE’s prestigious journals related to consumer technology.

The symposium will cover a broad range of topics, including but not limited to:

  • Sustainable Consumer Technologies
  • Smart Home Systems and IoT
  • Artificial Intelligence in Consumer Applications
  • Wearable and Health Monitoring Devices
  • Augmented and Virtual Reality
  • Advanced Imaging and Display Technologies
